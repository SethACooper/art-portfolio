Images and Notecards based on Pepper-Cooper's Emanuel Windows


In the Spring of 2004, Carol Pepper-Cooper was commissioned to design three 2 ½ X 6 ½ -- foot Stained Glass Windows for the Cantor Park Memorial Chapel in Kingston's Temple Emanuel. They were to represent, from left to right, Music, Justice, and Light, and were to be based on Scripture. They were completed late in 2004 and installed early in 2005.

Prints are available in various sizes showing all three windows together, as illustrated above, or any individual window of your choice. Available in addition are notecards showing the windows as illustrated above. For more information, please contact the artist.
